FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What skill level is required for your activities?

We offer activities for all skill levels, from beginner to expert. Each activity listing indicates the recommended skill level, and many include options for different experience levels.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

For guided experiences, all necessary equipment is provided and included in the price. For self-guided adventures, you can either bring your own gear or rent from our extensive collection of premium outdoor equipment.

Can I book activities for large groups?

Yes! We offer group bookings and custom experiences for teams, families, and gatherings. Contact our adventure specialists for special group rates and tailored itineraries.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 or more days before your scheduled activity receive a full refund. Cancellations within 3-6 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 3 days before are non-refundable, but we do offer the option to reschedule based on availability.
