Products

How do I choose the right tent size?

When choosing a tent, consider the following factors:

  • Number of people: Generally, add 1 to the number of people who will sleep in the tent. For example, a 3-person tent is comfortable for 2 people with gear.
  • Height: Check the peak height if you want to stand up inside.
  • Seasonality: 3-season tents are good for spring, summer, and fall, while 4-season tents are designed for winter conditions.
  • Weight: For backpacking, prioritize lightweight options.

You can find detailed sizing guides on each product page, or contact our customer service for personalized recommendations.

Are your products tested for extreme conditions?

Yes, all WildGear products undergo rigorous testing in various conditions before they reach the market. Our testing includes:

  • Lab testing for durability, waterproofing, and material strength
  • Field testing in extreme environments by professional outdoor enthusiasts
  • Long-term use testing to ensure product longevity

Our premium gear lines are tested in some of the world's most challenging environments, from the Himalayan mountains to Alaskan winters.

What's the difference between waterproof and water-resistant?

Water-resistant items can repel water to some degree but will eventually allow water to penetrate during prolonged exposure or heavy rain. These items are good for light rain or brief exposure to moisture.

Waterproof items are designed to keep water out completely under specified conditions. We measure waterproofing using a hydrostatic head rating (mm), which indicates how much water pressure the material can withstand before leaking.

All our waterproof products have sealed seams and water-tight zippers to prevent water from entering through these vulnerable areas.

Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days within the continental United States after your order ships. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout:

International shipping times vary by destination, typically ranging from 7-21 business days. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. During checkout, you'll see available shipping options and costs for your specific destination.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on unused merchandise. To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • In original condition with all tags attached
  • Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged
  • In original packaging

To initiate a return, log into your account and select the order you wish to return, or contact our customer service team. We'll provide a prepaid return shipping label for domestic returns. International returns are at the customer's expense.

How long does the warranty last?

Our warranty periods vary by product category:

  • Expedition Series gear: Lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects
  • Tents and shelters: 3-year warranty
  • Backpacks and bags: 2-year warranty
  • Apparel: 1-year warranty
  • Electronics and lighting: 1-year warranty

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover normal wear and tear, improper care, accidents, or modifications to the product. For warranty claims, please contact our customer service with proof of purchase and photos of the issue.

Do you offer repairs for damaged items?

Yes, we offer a repair service for items that have been damaged during normal use. Our repair options include:

  • Free repairs for manufacturing defects covered under warranty
  • Paid repairs for damage due to wear and tear or accidents
  • DIY repair kits for minor issues like small tears or zipper problems

To request a repair, contact our customer service team with a description and photos of the damage. We'll provide you with repair options, cost estimates, and shipping instructions.
