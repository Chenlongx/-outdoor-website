FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does the application process take?

The standard application review takes approximately 3 business days. Once approved, you'll receive your unique discount code and program instructions via email.

Can individual members of our organization use the discount?

Yes, once your organization is approved, we'll provide instructions on how individual members can access the discount. This typically involves verification of organization membership.

Are there any purchasing minimums?

There are no minimum purchase requirements for Bronze and Silver tier partners. Gold tier partners have a $1,000 annual minimum purchase requirement to maintain status.

Can we combine partner discounts with other promotions?

Partner discounts cannot be combined with other promotional discounts unless specifically stated. However, partners do receive exclusive access to special partner-only promotions throughout the year.

How often do we need to renew our partnership?

Partner status is valid for one year from the approval date. We'll send a renewal notification 30 days before expiration with a simple renewal process.
