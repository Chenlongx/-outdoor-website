Care Instructions

Extend the life of your gear with proper maintenance

GENERAL CARE TIPS
Proper care and maintenance help your gear stay reliable for every adventure.
  • Store gear clean and dry: Avoid mold and mildew buildup by drying before storage.
  • Avoid direct sunlight: UV exposure can degrade fabrics and plastics.
  • Clean zippers and seams: Use a soft brush or cloth to remove dirt that can cause wear.
  • Inspect regularly: Look for signs of wear or damage before use.
PRODUCT-SPECIFIC CARE
Here's how to care for specific gear categories:

Apparel

  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Avoid fabric softeners
  • Hang dry or tumble dry low
  • Reapply DWR coating periodically

Tents & Shelters

  • Spot clean with a damp cloth
  • Avoid harsh detergents
  • Air dry completely before storing
  • Store loosely rolled in a breathable bag

Sleeping Bags

  • Use a sleeping bag liner to reduce washing
  • Hand wash or use front-loading machine
  • Dry slowly with clean tennis balls to retain loft
  • Store loosely, never compressed long-term

Footwear

  • Brush off dirt after each use
  • Dry at room temperature (never use direct heat)
  • Condition leather occasionally
  • Use shoe trees to maintain shape
